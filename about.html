What we do ?

At Pesty, we believe that pets are full family members with whom we share our daily lives. They are there, always by our side, unconditionally, and we love them as they love us. Every year hundreds of animals such as dogs, cats become homeless for many reasons. Our mission :
is to find the homeless animals around the country and give them a better life. As like humans, they need love, care and also need home. We founded our organization to give them a chance for a loving home and family. Our successes are going forward and every year we give a better life to hundreds of homeless animals
Here you will find the pet you want, as well as everything you need to take care of it and live well with it.
Our experts bring you their advice for hygiene, feeding your animals and select the best accessories to take care of them.


Our Events

National Dog Training Day

This movement was created in collaboration with the association of dog trainers years ago. Take advantageof this month to teach your dog new tricks. This is an opportunity for trainers, canine experts and dog owners to celebrate their love for their companions.

National Day of Disabled Animals

Annually on May 3rd, National Specially-abled Pets Day shines a spotlight on the needs of pets with disabilities. This day is set aside to educate the public about caring for disabled pets. The day features disabled animals looking for a home.

The Woof Run

The Woof Run: The Woof Run is a day dedicated to dogs, on the shores of the lac1 . A course with human-dog pairs. After noon, our partners organize animations such as dog dancing The event is free for all spectators, and everyone can come with his or her dog(s)

Animal valentines day

For Valentine's Day, animals are also entitled to a little love! The Petsy website returns for the first edition of its annual collection. You will be able to offer hay or straw, accessories or even food for the animals in the shelters with-out any shipping costs.
